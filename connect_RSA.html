User Experince/ User Interface Design

Role: Sole UX Designer/Researcher

Platform: Television/Phone/Tablet

Timeframe: 12 Weeks

Solution

Connect is new way of communicating. A video chat service from your TV to connect you with you family and friends, all across the world. Connect focuses on people sharing experiences in life to create a better feeling of connectedness

01.

Research

This phase will follow research from the internet and gathered from users to help answer the question:

"How might we amplify the potential of entertainment technology to connect people to one another from the comfort and safety of home?"

Interviews

To gain insight on how to solve the issue, user interviews were conducted to assess how others were feeling during periods of social isolation

Empathy Maps

Findings from user interviews were collected and organised using an empathy map.

Affinity Diagrams

Points from the empathy map were grouped and collected based on cominality to later become pain points.

Key Research Findings

During the pandemic people were talking and reaching out more than ever before, with up to 90% of people saying it was essential for their mental health, but a number of problems occured for many of these people.

Price

27%

People who were worried that they would not be able to afford their broadband or bill pay plan to keep in touch with others.

Technology

34%

People who felt that they did not have the right technology in their home to communicate with the people they cared about.

Techreadiness

38%

People who felt they did not have the right technology to communicate with others or were worried about being left behind because of technology.

Pain Points

  • Large calls don't work. Small groups or 1-on-1 conversations work best
  • I need to reach out more
  • Quality time is essential
  • I need to keep myself occupied

User Goals

  • Avoid large group focus on smaller private conversations
  • Remove barriers for people trying to reach out
  • Create a place where people can be comfortable talking openly
  • Allow people to keep themselves occupied while communicating

02.

Prototyping

By creating user flows and wireframes a guideline can be created to match the new product vision:

"To bridge the gap of real conversation and quality time lost when communicating over technology"

User Journey Map

From our user goals, a journey map can be created that will satisfy those requirments. This map will show how the user will idealy feeling while using this product. This journey map follows a user asking to meet a friend for a chat using "connect" and later sitting down to enjoy some quality time over the app.

Wireframes

Using the journey map as a guide a rough flow of a product can be created that will meet the users needs and

Prototypes

Finshed pen & paper aswell as digital prototypes can be created from the previous wireframes. These can be shown to users for feedback.

03.

Testing

Using pen & paper and digital protypes that can be shown to users, feedback can easily be gathered on how to impove the user experience. This feedback can be used to then build and iterate the protype to something that closer resembles a finished product that meets the new product vision

Usability Testing

Usability testing puts prototypes in front of users to gather valuable feedback which can be used to further improve the product.

Test Findings

Feedback gathered from usability tests can be gathered grouped and decided what parts of the product needs iteration.

Prototype Iterations

Based on feedback iterations can be made to furter guide the project to a more polished finsihed product.

04.

Finished Design

From a culmination of all research, testing and iterations the finshed high fidelity User interface can be designed and finalised. For this project the finsihed design used a cross device app to allow users to use this service on existing technology but leave open the possibilty of future proofing the design.

Visual Identity

Using various fonts, images, designs and icons a visual brand that represents the theme and feeling of the product.

A/B Testing

Multiple designs can be made and shown to users for feedback, based on this the finished design will be one that will be the most desirable.

Hi-Fi Design

Finished designs can be created from a cumilation of all feedback.
