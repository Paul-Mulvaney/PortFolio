Sligo Chess Club

Graphic Design

Overview

To create a new Logo as well as branding and signage that would form the new visual identity for the Sligo Chess Club and their yearly tournament. The logo takes a modern and minimalist approach at a coat of arms, while encorprating elements from Sligos local landmarks and mythology. In keeping with the minimalist approch, branding was created to accompany the logo, using clean thin lines and a greyscale colour palette.

Design Keywords

Professional; Comfortable; Quality; Social; Ties to Sligo Area
