Problem

Product app and dashboard required an update to UX & UI to bring to modern standards, while being made of reusable and quickly made assets to have products ready for trial launch with Riot Games.

Solution

Update existing product application, while creating consistant modern design guidelines and reimagine user interactions to increase daily engagement. With this new app created a companion desktop dashboard administration ablication was also build to compliment.

Low Fidelity Prototyping

Due to time and budget constrains User research could not be conducted on the exising app before the redesign. To compensate for this, feedback from previous product trials was carefully analysed as well as conducting in house tests with the dev team and product owners.

Previous Product Trial Feedback

Feedback forms from previous were analysed to determine any issues that were common amonst users interating with the app.

Wireframing

To quickly create the rough outline of the application, extensive wireframing was conducted and tested on the dev team and product owner to ensure usabiity and viability of development on a tight timeframe.

Mid Fidelity Prototyping

Quick Prototyping

Following wireframes, simple mockups of the design were created which could be shown to dev team and product owner to ensure the flow up the application is working correctly and design is viable for development.
