Paul Mulvaney

User Experience / User Interface Designer

ATU Sligo Chess Club

Graphic Design

Overview

To create a new Logo as well as an advertisment to invite players to the weekly meeting at the college. The logo features a broad silhouette created by the shape of multiple chess pieces and a negative space cut out of the ATU Sligo campus fish statue. The poster is was designed to show the community aspects of the chess club to intice new players to join.

Design Keywords

Welcoming; Friendly; Approachable; Social; Connection to the University
